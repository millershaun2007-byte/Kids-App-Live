# Beta Testing Feedback Form

## How to Share This Beta

### Option 1: Share the Folder
1. Copy the entire `CProjectskids_learning_app` folder
2. Share via USB drive, email (zip it), or cloud storage
3. Recipients open `index.html` in their browser

### Option 2: Host Online (Recommended)
Once you're ready, you can host on:
- **Netlify** (free, easy drag-and-drop)
- **Vercel** (free, quick setup)
- **GitHub Pages** (free, requires git)

Then share the URL with testers!

## Tracking Your Beta Results

Create a simple spreadsheet with columns:
- Tester Name
- Child Age
- Feedback Date
- What They Liked
- What Needs Work
- Would Pay? (Y/N)
- Price They'd Pay
- Key Quote

**Goal:** Get 10-20 responses to see if there's real demand!
